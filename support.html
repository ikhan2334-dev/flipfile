Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first digital flipbook?
After signing up, go to your Dashboard and click 'Create New'. Upload your PDF file, customize the settings, and click 'Publish'. Your flipbook will be ready with a shareable link.
What file formats are supported?
We currently support PDF, PPT, PPTX, and DOCX files. For best results, we recommend using PDF files for consistent formatting.
Can I track who views my flipbook?
Yes! Our Pro and Business plans include detailed analytics that show you viewer counts, geographic location, and time spent on each page.
